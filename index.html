MISSION STATEMENT

Highlander Racing exists to foster professional relationships with global industry leaders as we build and maintain sponsor relations and our extensive alumni network. We aim to apply the knowledge we are taught in the classroom to develop our skills by overcoming challenges in real world scenarios.

OUR VISION

At UCR FSAE, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of EV technology and advancing toward autonomous racing. Our goal is to refine our design and manufacturing processes to maximize performance, reliability, and innovation. With each iteration of our EV platform, we aim to enhance efficiency, optimize testing timelines, and integrate cutting-edge autonomous capabilities.Through collaboration, hands-on experience, and continuous improvement, we strive to position UCR as a leading contender in Formula SAE, preparing our team for success in competition and beyond.

FORMULA SAE

Formula SAE is an international competition for student groups to design, manufacture, and race formula-style race cars. Each year our team of talented and passionate students plan, design, finance, and build a bespoke vehicle for a national level competition with a myriad of universities from around the world.
