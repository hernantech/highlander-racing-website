OUR SPONSORS

Our sponsors are exposed to over 25,000 of the best students, engineers, and faculty on campus, as well as other local students in STEM and racers at various SCCA and SAE SoCal events. Highlander Racing can creating an appropriate advertising solution to suit your needs. Your logo or name may be placed on our website (with link), car, team clothing, etc. Sponsors gain direct access to top-level students for co-ops, internships, and full time positions. Our partners are invited to special team and university events where you can see our progress and network with our team. Lastly, sponsors also receive our newsletters providing updates about team progress, events, and important news


Highlander Racing is a 501(c)(3) registered organization
